Learning Story

Our journey in coding started with learning how to structure and style a webpage. Understanding float, flexbox, and grid helped us create flexible layouts that work across devices. This is our story of how we explored new technologies, solved real-world problems, and developed essential coding skills to make a difference in our communities. We built websites, crafted applications, and created solutions that impacted not just ourselves, but everyone around us. Coding has empowered us to think creatively, solve complex problems, and continue pushing boundaries as women in tech.
